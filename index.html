Description

Drunk: Relapsed is a challenging and comedic 3D adventure. You will need to stumble and hop through the treacherous streets and alleys to reach the safety of your house. On your journey you'll be able to collect drinks to unlock new outfits.

History

After finishing another project we decided as a team that it would be fun to remake our own project. We had made a game called "Drunk and Reckless: Unforgiving Neighborhood Kilometer" in 2018. This idea was the birth of Drunk: Relapsed. Goal of the game was to create a product that would be better on every aspect compared to the predecessor. Here we are four months of development later ready to release our first Steam game as Team Tyrant.

Features

  • You need to get home safely after a wild night out. On the way, you will encounter many obstacles that will make your journey difficult.
  • Simple but hard to master. The maneuverability of the game is based on momentum, so be careful with your inputs. The game also features jumping, which requires perfect timing and coordination.
  • The game includes two game modes: Arcade & Endurance.
  • The slightest mistake will make you tumble down with ragdoll physics.
  • Compete against the world in Endurance mode using Steam leaderboards.
  • Collect golden bottles and take them to the next checkpoint to unlock new outfits!
  • Drunk: Relapsed fully supports controller and keyboard play.

About TeamTyrant
